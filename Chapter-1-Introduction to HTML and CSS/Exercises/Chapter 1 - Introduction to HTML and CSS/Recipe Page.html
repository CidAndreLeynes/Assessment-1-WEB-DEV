Cid's Favourite Recipe: Chocolate Chip Cookies

This is my favourite recipe! It's tasty, easy to make, and it always receives compliments from my friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt).
  3. In a separate mixing bowl, beat together the vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract until light and fluffy.
  4. Slowly add the liquid mixture to the dry mixture, stirring until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased and floured 9x13 inch baking pan.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
