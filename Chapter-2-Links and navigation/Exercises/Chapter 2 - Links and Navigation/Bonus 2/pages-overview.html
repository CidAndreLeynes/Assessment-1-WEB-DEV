Overview

Portal 2 is a first-person puzzle-platform video game developed and published by Valve. It is the sequel to the 2007 video game Portal and was released in April 2011 for Windows, PlayStation 3, and Xbox 360. The game was written by Erik Wolpaw and the Portal series' co-creator, Portal 2 picks up after Chell's victory over the rogue AI GLaDOS in the first game. Chell is awakened from stasis by an encouragement bot and must once again use the Aperture Science Handheld Portal Device (Portal Gun) to solve puzzles and escape the Aperture Science facility.
